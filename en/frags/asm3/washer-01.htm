Now we will wrap the washer body with an Assembly container.

  1. Switch to the Assembly 3 Workbench.
  1. Create a new Assembly, name it "Washer 000".
  2. Drag the washer Body on the Assembly node.
    The washer Body appears now under the Washer 000 > Parts group node.
  1. Click the menu Assembly3 > Create Assembly.
  2. Rename the new Assembly node to "Washer 000".
  3. Drag the Body on the Washer node.The body now appears under the Washer > Parts node.
  1. Collapse all child nodes.
